# Financial Commentary Templates

This reference provides structural templates for different styles of financial commentary.

## Template 1: Executive Summary Style

**Best for**: High-level overviews, quick reads, executive audiences

### Structure
```markdown
# [Company] Q[Quarter] [Year] Earnings Review

## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentence overview of quarter performance]
- Revenue: [amount] ([growth]% YoY)
- EPS: [amount] vs [consensus]
- Key takeaway: [one-line summary]

## Financial Highlights
### Revenue
- [amount] ([growth]% YoY / [growth]% QoQ)
- Driven by [segment / product / region]

### Profitability
- Gross Margin: [%] ([change]bps YoY)
- Operating Margin: [%] ([change]bps YoY)
- EPS: [amount] ([growth]% YoY)

## Segment Performance
### [Segment A]
- Revenue: [amount] ([growth]% YoY)
- Key drivers: [brief explanation]

### [Segment B]
- Revenue: [amount] ([growth]% YoY)
- Key drivers: [brief explanation]

## Outlook & Guidance
Management guided to:
- Revenue: [range]
- EPS: [range]
- Key assumptions: [brief]

## Investment Thesis
[3-4 bullet points on bull/bear case]
- ✓ [Positive factor]
- ⚠ [Risk factor]
```

## Template 2: Detailed Analysis Style

**Best for**: Deep dives, research reports, thorough reviews

### Structure
```markdown
# [Company] Q[Quarter] [Year] Detailed Analysis

## Overview
[Paragraph summarizing the quarter, comparing to expectations, and setting tone for analysis]

## Revenue Analysis
### Top-Line Performance
Revenue of [amount] represented a [growth]% YoY increase, [above/below] consensus of [amount].
The growth was driven by [primary driver], partially offset by [secondary factor].

### Revenue Mix
| Segment | Revenue | YoY Growth | Margin |
|---------|---------|------------|--------|
| [A]     | [amt]   | [%]        | [%]    |
| [B]     | [amt]   | [%]        | [%]    |

[Analysis of segment performance and mix shifts]

## Profitability Analysis
### Gross Margin
Gross margin of [%] [expanded/compressed] by [change]bps YoY to [%].
[Explanation: pricing, mix, cost inflation, etc.]

### Operating Expenses
Operating expenses of [amount] grew [growth]% YoY, faster/slower than revenue.
- SG&A: [amount] ([growth]% YoY) - [explanation]
- R&D: [amount] ([growth]% YoY) - [explanation]

### Operating & Net Income
- Operating Income: [amount] ([growth]% YoY)
- Operating Margin: [%] ([change]bps YoY)
- Net Income: [amount] ([growth]% YoY)
- Net Margin: [%] ([change]bps YoY)

## Balance Sheet & Cash Flow
### Balance Sheet
- Cash: [amount] [change from last quarter]
- Debt: [amount] [change from last quarter]
- Net Cash/Debt: [amount]

### Cash Flow
- Operating Cash Flow: [amount]
- Free Cash Flow: [amount]
- CapEx: [amount]

## Management Commentary
[Key quotes or paraphrased points from earnings call]

### Strategic Priorities
1. [Priority 1] - [brief update]
2. [Priority 2] - [brief update]
3. [Priority 3] - [brief update]

## Outlook
### Guidance
Management raised/maintained/lowered guidance for [year]:
- Revenue: [range] (consensus: [amount])
- EPS: [range] (consensus: [amount])

### Key Assumptions
- [Assumption 1]
- [Assumption 2]

## Risks & Opportunities
### Risks
- [Risk 1]: [brief explanation]
- [Risk 2]: [brief explanation]

### Opportunities
- [Opportunity 1]: [brief explanation]
- [Opportunity 2]: [brief explanation]

## Conclusion
[Paragraph summarizing investment view and recommendation]
```

## Template 3: Comparative Analysis Style

**Best for**: Peer comparisons, relative performance focus

### Structure
```markdown
# [Company] Q[Quarter] [Year]: Peer Comparison

## Performance Snapshot
| Metric | [Company] | [Peer 1] | [Peer 2] |
|--------|-----------|----------|----------|
| Revenue Growth | [%] | [%] | [%] |
| Margin | [%] | [%] | [%] |
| EPS Growth | [%] | [%] | [%] |

## Revenue Growth
[Company]'s [growth]% revenue growth [outperformed/trailed] peers, which averaged [avg]%.
[Explanation of why company differed]

## Profitability
[Company]'s [margin]% operating margin [exceeded/lagged] peer average of [avg]%.
[Explanation of margin differences]

## Valuation
| Multiple | [Company] | Peer Avg |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| P/E ( fwd) | [x] | [x] |
| EV/EBITDA | [x] | [x] |
| P/B | [x] | [x] |

[Analysis on valuation and whether premium/discount is warranted]
```

## Template 4: Thematic Analysis Style

**Best for**: Focus on specific themes, strategic shifts, turnaround stories

### Structure
```markdown

## Thesis
[Statement of the investment thesis or theme being analyzed]

## Evidence This Quarter
### [Theme Point 1]
[Data and analysis supporting this aspect of the thesis]

### [Theme Point 2]
[Data and analysis supporting this aspect of the thesis]

### Counterarguments
[Data or points that contradict the thesis]
[How management addresses these concerns]

## Progress Tracking

|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| [KPI 1] | [val] | [val] | [↗/→/↘] |
| [KPI 2] | [val] | [val] | [↗/→/↘] |

## What to Watch Next Quarter
- [Item 1]
- [Item 2]

[Assessment of thesis progression]
```

## Style Variations

### Conservative Analyst Style
**Characteristics**: Cautious tone, balanced presentation, emphasis on risks

**Language patterns**:
- "While revenue growth was strong, margins..."
- "We remain cautious on [issue]..."
- "Execution remains a key risk..."
- "Valuation appears full at current levels..."

### Growth-Oriented Style
**Characteristics**: Focus on growth metrics, market opportunity, less emphasis on near-term profitability

**Language patterns**:
- "Strong top-line momentum continues..."
- "Market opportunity remains significant..."
- "Investments driving long-term growth..."
- "Path to profitability becoming clearer..."

### Value Style
**Characteristics**: Focus on cash flows, asset value, capital allocation

**Language patterns**:
- "Free cash flow generation remains robust..."
- "Balance sheet provides optionality..."
- "Capital allocation prioritized shareholder returns..."
- "Trading below intrinsic value..."

### Turnaround Style
**Characteristics**: Focus on operational improvements, strategic shifts, stabilization

**Language patterns**:
- "Early signs of stabilization..."
- "Restructuring efforts showing progress..."
- "New strategic initiatives gaining traction..."
- "Path to sustainable profitability emerging..."

## Common Opening Hooks

1. **The "Expectation" Hook**
   "Company X reported Q3 results that [exceeded/fell short of] market expectations..."

2. **The "Theme" Hook**
   "This quarter marked a [pivotal/challenging/transformative] period for Company X as..."

3. **The "Comparison" Hook**
   "Company X's results [contrasted with/mirrored] those of its peers, with..."

4. **The "Story" Hook**
   "The narrative this quarter centered on [theme], as Company X..."

5. **The "Highlight" Hook**
   "Headline the quarter: [key metric], which [significance]..."

## Common Closing Structures

1. **The "Balanced View" Closing**
   "On balance, [positive aspects] offset by [concerns]. We remain [rating]..."

2. **The "Watch List" Closing**
   "Heading into next quarter, we'll be focused on [1], [2], and [3]..."

3. **The "Action" Closing**
   "Given [factors], we are [upgrading/downgrading/initiating] to [rating]..."

4. **The "Question" Closing**
   "The key question for investors: [question about future performance]..."

5. **The "Thesis" Closing**
   "[Thesis statement]. We'll look for [evidence] in coming quarters to support this view."
